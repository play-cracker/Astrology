Panchangams in Astrology

  • Tithi (Lunar Day): A lunar day defined as the time it takes for the Moon to move 12 degrees from the Sun. It carries unique energies that affect emotional and spiritual well-being.
  • Vara (Weekday): Days of the week ruled by specific planets, influencing activities and important for decision-making.
  • Nakshatra (Lunar Mansion): 27 lunar mansions with distinct traits impacting personality, compatibility, and timing for various life events.
  • Yoga (Combination of Tithi and Vara): Indicates the overall energy of a moment, helping determine auspicious timings for activities.
  • Karana (Half of a Tithi): Provides insights into shorter time frames, helping fine-tune daily actions based on cosmic energies.
